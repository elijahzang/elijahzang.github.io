Interactive resume With Power Bi

Visualization Feb 02, 2020

  • Elie Charles Zang

    Data analyst | Business Intelligence Developper

Data is everywhere these days, but it’s what we do with that data, the insights we tap into that make it so valuable. And that is the reason why visualization in general and data storytelling in particular is so important.


Don’t we say that: 
“a picture is worth a thousand words”

According this idiom, is often easier to show something in a picture than to describe it with words. The way of storytelling has changed and it is evolving.  Now more company are using visualization tools to present their information. 
Rather than presenting a visualization, focus on a company or a marketing campaign performance, I will introduce an interactive resume using Power Bi.

The final result looks great and it combines personal information such as: my work experience, my education and my skills. Company names and skills are interactive. So, you can view skills by companies and also be able to see some mains tools used by skills. Please find the link to interactive report:
